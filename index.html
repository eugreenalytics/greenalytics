What is euGreenalytics

euGreenalytics is an independent platform dedicated to analysing and visualising energy data, with a special focus on solar photovoltaic developments across Europe. The mission is to transform complex datasets into clear, accessible and actionable insights — from installed capacity trends to electricity markets, technology comparisons, and system flexibility.

The platform brings together interactive dashboards, analytical content, and research-driven energy intelligence to help professionals, researchers, and energy enthusiasts better understand the European energy transition.

About

euGreenalytics is an independent, non-commercial initiative created to make energy data more accessible and understandable. The project focuses on providing transparent analysis, intuitive visualisations and evidence-based interpretations of Europe's rapidly evolving energy landscape.

The aim is to support better decision-making — from industry professionals to curious citizens — through clear, unbiased and well-structured information. While the platform remains anonymous for now, it reflects years of experience working with energy datasets, photovoltaic technologies and electricity market dynamics.

Consulting support may be available for organisations needing tailored data analysis or custom dashboards.
